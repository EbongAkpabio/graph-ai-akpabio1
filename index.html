About Me

I'm a medical doctor, public health specialist, and independent consultant with over 25 years of experience leading health and development programs across Africa. My work focuses on strengthening health systems through monitoring & evaluation, capacity building, and evidence-driven interventions. I've served as a lead consultant for Namibia's Ministry of Health and Social Services, the World Health Organization, UNFPA, and the Global Fund, and I currently advise regional malaria elimination initiatives.

I hold an MSc in Epidemiology (London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine), an MBA (MANCOSA), and a PhD in Public Health. Passionate about human-centred innovation, I am exploring how generative AI can amplify research, improve clinical outcomes, and empower communities.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research – Productivity Bootcamp (Q2 2025) is an immersive 8-week program that equips professionals with hands-on skills to:

  • Create polished websites, documents, and presentations with AI assistance
  • Conduct literature reviews and build interactive dashboards without coding
  • Design custom chatbots for domain-specific support
  • Automate workflows that run 24/7
  • Generate and edit images, videos, and design assets
  • Integrate practical AI solutions into daily projects for measurable impact

By the end of the program, participants master strategies to save time, elevate quality, and tackle real-world challenges across research, creative industries, and business contexts.
